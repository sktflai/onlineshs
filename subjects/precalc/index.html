Pre-Calculus (Grade 11 STEM)

Subject Overview: This course focuses on analytic geometry, conic sections, series and sequences, and mathematical induction. It prepares students for calculus by strengthening algebraic manipulation and geometric understanding.

Course Learning Objectives

  • Illustrate and identify the different conic sections (circle, parabola, ellipse, hyperbola).
  • Determine the standard and general equations of conic sections.
  • Solve situational problems involving conic sections.
  • Understand sequences, series, and the binomial theorem.
  • Apply mathematical induction to prove statements.
