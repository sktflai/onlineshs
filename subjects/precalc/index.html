Unit 1: Analytic Geometry

Circle

Objectives: Identify standard/general form, find center/radius, graph.

Standard: (x - h)² + (y - k)² = r²

Example: (x + 4)² + (y - 1)² = 25 → Center (-4,1), Radius 5

Video: Khan Academy Circle

Parabola

Forms: (x - h)² = 4p(y - k) or (y - k)² = 4p(x - h)

Example: (x - 1)² = -8(y + 2) → Vertex (1,-2), opens down

Video: Parabola

Ellipse & Hyperbola

Ellipse: (x-h)²/a² + (y-k)²/b² = 1

Hyperbola: (x-h)²/a² - (y-k)²/b² = 1
