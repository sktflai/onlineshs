Pre-Calculus Index

Course Overview: This covers analytic geometry, symmetries, tessellations, the golden ratio, Fibonacci sequences, matrices, and linear programming as per the Philippine Grade 11 STEM curriculum.

Unit 1: Symmetries and Transformations

Learning Objectives: Understand types of symmetries, transformations, and their applications.

Unit 2: Tessellations and Frieze Patterns

Learning Objectives: Explore tiling patterns, frieze groups, and real-world designs.

Unit 3: Golden Ratio and Fibonacci Sequence

Learning Objectives: Study the golden ratio, Fibonacci numbers, and their occurrences in nature/art.

Unit 4: Matrices and Linear Programming

Learning Objectives: Master matrix operations, systems of equations, and optimization problems.
