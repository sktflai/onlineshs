Pre-Calculus (Grade 11 STEM)

Subject Overview: This course introduces analytic geometry, focusing on conic sectionsâ€”their definitions, equations, graphs, and real-world applications. It strengthens algebraic and geometric skills essential for calculus.

Course Learning Objectives

  • Illustrate the formation of conic sections from a double cone.
  • Derive, transform, and graph standard/general equations of circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas.
  • Determine key elements (center, vertices, foci, directrix, asymptotes).
  • Solve situational problems involving conic sections.
  • Recognize degenerate cases and solve systems of conics.
