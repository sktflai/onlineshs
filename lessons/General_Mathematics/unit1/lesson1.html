Lesson 1: Review of Functions

Unit 1: Functions and Their Graphs | General Mathematics - Grade 11 STEM

Practice Exercises

Test your understanding. Try solving before checking answers.

  1. Determine whether the following relation is a function: {(3,5), (4,6), (3,7), (5,8)}.
  2. If f(x) = 2x + 3, find: f(0), f(5), f(-2), and f(a + 1).
  3. State the domain and range of the function shown in a graph of y = √(x - 1).
  4. Use the vertical line test mentally: Is a circle a function? Why or why not?
  5. Identify the type of function: f(x) = 4 (constant or linear?)
Answers:

1. Not a function (x=3 has two y-values: 5 and 7)
2. f(0)=3, f(5)=13, f(-2)=-1, f(a+1)=2(a+1)+3=2a+5
3. Domain: x ≥ 1, Range: y ≥ 0
4. No — a vertical line intersects a circle at two points
5. Constant function (horizontal line)
