Unit 4: Review and Applications of Conic Sections

Learning Objectives

  • Solve systems involving conics (intersection points).
  • Identify degenerate conics.
  • Apply conics to real-world situations.

Degenerate Cases

When the plane passes through the vertex: point, line, or intersecting lines.

Systems and Applications

Find intersection of line and circle (tangent/secant), or circle and parabola.

General conic equation: Ax² + Bxy + Cy² + Dx + Ey + F = 0 (B² - 4AC determines type).

Interesting Facts

  • Conics appear in architecture (parabolic arches), physics (trajectories), astronomy.
  • All conics are related by projective geometry.
