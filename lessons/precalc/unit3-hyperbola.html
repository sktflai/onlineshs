Unit 3: Hyperbola

Learning Objectives

  • Derive standard equation of hyperbola.
  • Identify center, vertices, foci, asymptotes.
  • Graph hyperbolas.

Detailed Explanation

Difference of distances to two foci is constant. Eccentricity e > 1.

Standard forms:

  • Horizontal: (x - h)²/a² - (y - k)²/b² = 1
  • Vertical: (y - k)²/a² - (x - h)²/b² = 1

Examples

Example: x²/9 - y²/16 = 1 → a=3, b=4, c=5, foci (±5, 0).

Interesting Facts

  • Hyperbolas have two branches.
  • Applications: Navigation (LORAN), nuclear cooling towers, comet orbits.
